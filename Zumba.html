STEPS:

Basic Salsa

1. stand with both feet together.

2. Step to the right.Bend Your Knees.

3. return to the middle.

4. Step to the left.Bend your knees slightly.

5. Return to the middle.

6. Try a little faster.Slightly rock to the side as you move.
Loosen up your upper body.Let your shoulder move to feet.

7. Faster this Time!

Travel Salsa

1. Bend your knees.Slightly bend arms.Point them to the right towards the sky.

2. Take four steps to the right side use your hips to move.

3.Return to the middle.

4. Repeat steps 1.This time points arms to the left.

5.Take four step to the left use your hips to move.

7. Faster this Time!
